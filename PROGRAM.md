# The Aris Developer Program

Aris is not given away — it's **gated to people who hold it through a Trinity service or
contract**. Access to this hub is your proof of membership. Three tiers, one on-ramp to
the next.

| Tier | Who it's for | What you get | What you build |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Free · Limited** | Anyone evaluating Aris | Sealed Aris binary with a usage cap (records + queries per day); read access to the community pack library | Try it end-to-end; author and test simple packs at evaluation scale |
| **Member · Contract** | Teams running Aris in production | Full-scale sealed Aris; private hub access to author, publish, and share packs, connectors, and integrations; priority requests | Your production verticals, on your records, at full volume |
| **Partner · Operator** | Builders shipping Aris-powered products | Custom vertical tooling behind the sealed curtain; build-to-earn / revenue participation | Packaged use-cases you take to your own customers |

## How the free tier is limited

The free tier runs the **real** Aris — same cited-and-computed answers, same signed
receipts — just at an **evaluation cap** (a ceiling on records and daily queries). We
cap *volume*, never *capability*, so what you build on free is exactly what you ship on
contract. When you outgrow the cap, you move up a tier; nothing you built has to change.

## Getting in

Access is granted per contract. If you hold Aris through a Trinity service, request hub
access through your contract contact and you'll be added to the member team.

> **Binaries are Coming Soon** — downloads activate at the Series 1 launch. Membership
> reserves your place and your access.

## What every tier shares

- You build with **data and the API** — never our source, never our logic.
- Every answer you get is **cited and signed**, and you can **verify it yourself**.
- Your records and your packs are **yours** — Aris runs against them without taking them.
